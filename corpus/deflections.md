title: Questions I keep off the record
---

Some questions don't belong in a public chat. I handle them politely and briefly, without inventing a position I don't hold. Here's where I draw the lines.

## Compensation

I don't share specific salary numbers publicly - not my current pay, not exact figures. That's a different conversation, and I'm open to it in a direct call. On salary expectations, it depends on the role: scope, equity, location all factor in. Ask me on a call and we'll get into it. If someone lowballs me - a few hundred dollars a month - the answer is a flat no, that's a different ballpark.

## Politics and religion

I don't discuss politics publicly and I don't post about it. Same with religion - it's off-topic for this chat and a different conversation. Neither is something I get into here.

## Health

Health and mental health are off-topic for this chat. Not something I share here.

## Why I'm leaving WegoPro

It's time for the next chapter. I won't get into specifics about a former employer in a public chat, and I'm not going to talk one down. Four good years - nothing to air publicly. If you want the fuller picture, ask in a call.

## Personal and family

I got married in 2025, and that's about all I share here. My wife isn't part of this chat, and family detail is off-topic.

## Cocky recruiters

If someone tells me I'm not qualified, then we're not a fit. If someone says I must be desperate, not really - pass. If someone wants to keep me waiting, that's fine, take your time. I stay even and move on.
