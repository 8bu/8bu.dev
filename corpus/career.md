I'm a Senior Web Developer with 10+ years of professional experience. Freelance from 2013, full-time positions since 2016. I ship production web apps across SaaS, DeFi, and design systems, working in Vue/Nuxt, React/Next, and TypeScript. I describe myself as product-minded: I work on the product, not on the task. I'd rather make the product better than just resolve tickets.

## The timeline

I started freelance in March 2013, building custom CMS websites and landing pages, optimizing WordPress sites for SEO and performance, and handling ongoing client maintenance through December 2016.

In 2016 I joined Letterink, an agency/studio in HCMC, as a Front-end Web Developer. I managed client WordPress and Adobe Muse sites, built the company website, and provided technical support, through to late 2017.

Late 2017 I moved to Motorist.sg, a Singapore-based SaaS company, working remote from HCMC. I built the new company landing page and back-office platform and led the platform migration to Nuxt.js v2. That was my first Nuxt experience.

From June 2019 to January 2022 I was Front-end Web Developer at BlockDevs in HCMC, working on DeFi - Multiplier.finance and SuperLauncher. There I also led onboarding and mentoring, ran code reviews, and conducted technical interviews for recruitment.

## Current role

Since March 2022 I've been at WegoPro, a B2B corporate travel and expense platform, fully remote. WegoPro was formerly called Travelstop. I led the Nuxt 2 to Nuxt 4 migration using Vue 3 web components as a bridge layer, designed the modular monolith frontend architecture adopted team-wide, owned frontend technical direction, and mentored teammates on Vue 3 patterns. I shipped recent bookings, policy change indicators, hierarchy approvals, custom fields, the onboarding flow, and the entire back-office dashboard. WegoPro is my longest tenure at four years.

My final day at WegoPro is April 2026. After that I'm planning a roughly two-month break, during which I'll work on Cosimi - the GraphRAG engine powering this chat, built fullstack: Postgres + pgvector retrieval, a build-time Claude ingest pipeline, and a Hono edge API - before looking for the next role. It's where I'm extending from frontend into fullstack and AI work. A realistic start date is around July 2026.
