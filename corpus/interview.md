A few things an interviewer asks that aren't on my CV - what I'm after next, and how I get into unfamiliar work.

## What I'm looking for next

I finish at WegoPro in April 2026 and take a short break to build Cosimi before my next role. What I want next is a senior frontend role where I own technical direction and work on the product rather than a ticket queue - Vue/Nuxt or React/Next, on a team that takes craft and migrations seriously. I'm open to early conversations now.

## How I approach a new codebase

I read before I touch. I map the seams - where state lives, where the boundaries already are - then make the smallest change that proves the path, and only then scale it out. It's the same instinct that makes live migrations work: modernize underneath without halting delivery.

## Why remote

I've worked fully remote since 2017 - Motorist from Ho Chi Minh City, then BlockDevs and WegoPro. I'm on GMT+7 and deliver async-first. Remote isn't a perk for me, it's how I've shipped for most of my career.
