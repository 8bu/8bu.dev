Both fonts ship under the SIL Open Font License 1.1. Bundling + redistribution in compiled artifacts (PNG og:image cards) is permitted. Full OFL text: https://scripts.sil.org/cms/scripts/page.php?item_id=OFL_web

These files MUST support Vietnamese combining marks (ễ, ữ, ờ, ...) - the watermark "Long NGUYỄN (8bu)" relies on them. Verified at I7 generation time by visual inspection of the rendered PNG; if any glyph renders as tofu, swap the font.
