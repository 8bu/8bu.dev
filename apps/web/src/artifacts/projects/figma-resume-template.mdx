## What it is

A clean, minimal résumé / CV / cover-letter template I designed and published on the Figma Community. Built entirely with auto layout, for developers, designers, and product folks who want a no-nonsense CV that's easy to edit and adapt.

## What's in it

- A résumé page and a matching cover-letter page, one consistent system.
- Fully auto-laid-out - add or remove a role, a bullet, a section, and everything reflows. No manual nudging.
- A restrained type scale and spacing rhythm that stays legible in print and on screen.
- Swappable accents so you can make it yours without breaking the layout.

## Why I made it

I wanted my own CV to be trivial to keep current, so I built the system instead of a one-off document - then published it for anyone who wants the same. It's a small, focused piece of design work: layout discipline, typographic restraint, and auto layout doing the heavy lifting.
