## What it is

NTWRX is a Web3 creator-and-brand community - it connects influencers with brands to run social-media marketing campaigns, then rewards engagement on-chain. Browse campaigns, post and engage across platforms, and earn `$NTWRX` based on how your content performs. Currently in beta. I worked on it as designer and web developer.

## What I shipped

The creator-facing dapp, design and front-end:

- Campaign discovery - browse opportunities posted by brands and other users.
- Cross-platform engagement tracking - performance pulled from Instagram, X, and other networks.
- Token-reward surfaces - `$NTWRX` earned against post metrics (views, likes, shares).
- A quest-creation studio for brands to design custom engagement campaigns.
- The on-chain wiring - wallet connect and smart-contract reads/writes for transparent, automated reward distribution.

## How I worked

- Designer and web dev - I took screens from design through to shipped UI.
- Held a consistent dark, Web3-native visual language across the product.
- Built the front end on Nuxt + Vue 3 + Tailwind, integrating the Ethereum contract layer.

## Stack details

TypeScript, Nuxt, Vue 3, Tailwind. Web3: Ethereum smart contracts for campaign tracking and reward payouts. reCAPTCHA for abuse protection; Cloudflare for delivery and analytics. Design in Figma.
